Why Use CanadianVisa.Org?

With hundreds of immigration agencies available, it can be quite difficult to decide on which consultancy to use. Immigration scams are on the rise, and finding a trustworthy and knowledgeable immigration company that will give you the best possible chances to live your Canadian dream is not that easy. So with so many options available to you, all claiming to make the application process simple, let's take a look at why you should use CanadianVisa.org and what sets us apart from the rest.

Reasons to Use Our Immigration Services

Digital Visa Dashboard

Digital Visa Dashboard

By using our services you will have access to your very own state-of-the-art online personal dashboard or Digital Visa Dashboard. What does this mean for you? No more lengthy confusing government forms. Upload your information and supporting documents directly to your profile and track your visa application progress in real-time, all from the comfort of your own home. You will also have direct contact with your very own dedicated RCIC through a built-in ticketing system and will be kept up-to-date on your visa status every step of the way so that you can rest easy knowing that your application is in good hands.

Dedicated RCIC

As mentioned earlier you will have a dedicated RCIC who will be handling your application. RCICs, or Regulated Canadian Immigration Consultants, are the only licensed professionals, along with immigration lawyers, who are permitted to offer paid visa services. The RCICs that we work with have years of experience and are knowledgeable of the latest immigration news and policies. These RCICs have also been through the immigration process themselves and are motivated to help make your dreams of moving to Canada a reality.

Unlike other Canadian immigration agencies, you will be able to contact your RCIC directly via your personal Visa Profile Builder with any queries or concerns that you may have.

Quicker Turnaround Time

If you plan to invest in your future by studying in Canada, youll need to apply for a study permit. On top of college and university submissions, having to submit visa applications and documents too can be quite stressful, not to mention choosing the best educational institution for you. Our services include guidance and support from your own student advisor who will not only help you complete your college submission but your motivation letter too, and once youve been accepted, we'll take care of your study permit too. We are also affiliated with Evergreen College in Toronto, Ontario which has a variety of courses to choose from and youll also benefit from discounted tuition fees.

Personal Student Advisor

The application process can be quite a lengthy one, averaging anywhere from 6 - 18 months, depending on the immigration program. This is due to various reasons and varies on a case-by-case basis, however, the main reason is that many people are still submitting paper applications or, even though many companies offer online services, they may still have to send your supporting documents and applications by mail or courier. By using our online Visa Profile Builder you can upload everything directly to the portal and because our RCICs are located in Canada we can get your application submitted quicker than most.
