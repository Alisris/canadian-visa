Labour Day Weekend Getaways for Students in Canada

by Nicky VZ. Updated: September 2nd, 2020

Students in Canada make the most of Labour Day weekend to finish summer off with a bang! And who doesnt love a long weekend getaway? Its the perfect time to unwind, relieve some pent-up exam stress and simply have a good time with friends and family. Whether youre a nature lover or city slicker - theres something for everyone to do and see in Canada.

Keep reading to find out where students travel in Canada to spend their Labour Day weekends.

Labour Day Weekend Camping Trips


Camping on Labour Day weekend is a classic Canadian thing to do! Think roasted marshmallows and ghost stories around campfires, hiking, canoeing, and hotdogs by the lake with your friends. And luckily, a camping trip is both affordable and adventure-filled. So, where to travel in Canada to find the perfect spot to set up camp?

The east coast has no shortage of amazing camping spots with spectacular sea views. The Corney Brook Campground in Nova Scotia lies along the Cabot Trail that offers picturesque views of the coastal landscape. The Chignecto Campground is situated inland from the famous Bay of Fundy and comes complete with amenities such as showers, kitchen shelters, and even Wi-Fi to share your epic views with friends on the Gram!


Mount Robson provincial park in British Columbia is a campsite of dreams. Youll be enjoying your morning coffee surrounded by snow-capped peaks next to crystal blue waters. If you study at the U of T, then youll be able to make a quick getaway to St. Lawrence Park, pitch a tent or hire a cabin.

Labour Day Weekend in Toronto


Labour day weekend in Toronto comes packed with fun and exciting festivals and activities for every type of student. Comic fans can meet and take photos with their favorite superheroes and anime characters at the Fan Expo. There are tons of interactive shows and prices up for grabs for the best cosplay outfits!

If youd rather spend your Labour Day Weekend outside soaking up the last bit of warmth then youll enjoy the Food Truck Festival at Canadas Wonderland Theme Park. You get to sample delicious food from over 15 different food trucks and ride on the Yukon Striker, the worlds tallest and longest dive roller coaster.

Electric music fans will get a kick out of the Electric Island music event that takes place on the Toronto Islands. Electronic musicians from around the world perform during this 2-day event!

Labour Day Weekend in Whistler


If you want to travel in Canada for Labour Day weekend then you simply must visit Whistler - a small town north of Vancouver thats a recreation paradise. Its the perfect getaway for a couple or a couple of friends to take in the breathtaking views from the Gondola lift and partake in a variety of executions and activities.

The Whistler website offers great package deals on accommodation and summer activities like bear viewing tours, ziplining, river rafting, spa treatments and so much more! Whats a Labour Day Weekend without overindulging? Whistler has some of the best restaurants and food markets in all of Canada. Pizza, cheesy burgers, fillet mignon, sushi, truffle fries, and craft beer - something to satisfy your cravings and suit every type of budget.

Interested in Studying in Canada?


Thousands of international students study in Canada every year. Canada offers affordable study options, easy application processes, ample employment opportunities for foreign graduates, and of course amazing places to travel in Canada to get the best student experience possible!
