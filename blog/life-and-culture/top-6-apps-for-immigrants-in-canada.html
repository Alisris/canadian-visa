Top 4 Apps for Immigrants in Canada

by Nicky VZ. Updated: June 30th, 2021

If youre a newcomer to Canada theres an app for that.

Dont be left in the dark when you can quite literally find all the information you need to kick-start your new life in Canada at your fingertips. Whether youre looking for a job, a new home, schools or even scholarships to study in Canada, youll most likely be able to download a handy app to help you find what you need.

Get a headstart with our list of top 4 apps for immigrants in Canada.

Top 4 Apps for Newcomers to Canada

1. ArriveCAN App


As part of Canada's efforts to reduce the spread of COVID-19 and prevent importation, all travelers are required to digitally provide contact and quarantine information upon and after entry into Canada through the ArriceCAN app. Users of the app say that it helps with quicker processes at the border. Everything was fine at the border, the agent accessed fast all the information that I have provided, and I am happy the app contributed for fast and easy processing.

2. Jobs in Canada App


Its no secret that the biggest challenge for newcomers is finding jobs before or even after theyve settled in Canada. And thats why one of the top apps for immigrants in Canada is Jobs in Canada. The app gathers all the best industry jobs right across the country from popular job sites like Careerbuilder, WowJobs, Monster, and Indeed in one place, making it easier to apply on multiple sites without creating multiple accounts!

The app is rated 4.1 out of 5 on Google Play. I really love this app, help people to get a good job of their choice in Canada. Keep it up, wrote one user.

3.TripAdvisor


Downloaded and used by millions of travelers around the world, Tripadvisor is a great app for new immigrants in Canada who are unfamiliar with their surroundings. It helps you to discover traveler-recommended hotels, restaurants, tours, attractions, and other experiences nearby. You can also conveniently book stayovers, tickets and reserve tables directly on the app.

Tripadvisor allows you to make educated decisions about where you go with the help of genuine reviews and feedback from previous visitors. Reading reviews before we go really helps us plan our trips, wrote a user. The app is rated 4.4 out of 5 by 1,390,644 users.

4. Canadian Citizenship Test 2023


Permanent residents can apply for Canadian citizenship after approximately three years or less. To become a permanent resident you must pass a citizenship test. The test assesses your rights and responsibilities as a Canadian citizen and Canadas history, geography, economy, government, laws, and symbols. The Canadian Citizenship Test 2023 app makes studying for your citizenship test fun with interactive features and audio lessons! With over 600 questions and 30 practice tests, you will be well-prepared to ace your test.

So glad I found this app! Everything is broken down into chapters and bite-size lessons, wrote a user.

Planning on Moving to Canada?


Are you planning on taking the plunge and moving to the number one country in the world, eh? Canada is a top immigrant destination and thats why there is no shortage of incredible apps for immigrants in Canada designed to make the transition to a new life a little easier.

If everything goes as planned Canada will be welcoming 1.23 million newcomers by 2023! The substantial immigration target was created to help alleviate the skills and labour shortages across the country as well as to boost the Canadian economy with more skilled workers and entrepreneurs.

With over 100 different visa and immigration programs available, you stand a great chance of qualifying for at least one of them! Learn a few quick facts about the top immigration programs in Canada below.

Top Canadian Immigration Programs

  • Express Entry System;
  • Provincial Nomination Programs;
  • Family Sponsorship; and
  • Atlantic Immigration Pilot

Express Entry System

Skilled foreign workers can apply for permanent residency from outside of Canada through the Express Entry system. The system manages three immigration programs that will admit 108,500 skilled immigrants in 2023. One of the many benefits of taking the Express route to Canada is that you could obtain a permanent resident visa in as little as six months!

Provincial Nomination Program

Skilled and intermediate-skilled foreign workers could earn provincial nominations to apply for permanent residency in Canada if their skills are in demand in one of the eleven participating provinces. There are over 80 immigration streams welcoming thousands of newcomers every year.

Family Sponsorship

Canada cares about keeping families together. If you are successful in your immigration journey and become a permanent resident of Canada then you could sponsor your nearest and dearest to join you in the Great North through the Family Sponsorship Program.

Atlantic Immigration Pilot

Fancy living near the coast? Canadas four Atlantic provinces have their own special immigration pilot program known as the Atlantic Immigration Pilot. Skilled and intermediate-skilled foreign workers who manage to secure employment in one of these regions could apply for permanent residency.
