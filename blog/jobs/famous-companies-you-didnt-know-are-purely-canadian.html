Famous Companies You Didn't Know Are Purely Canadian

by Theresa. Updated: June 17th, 2021

It’s no secret that Canada has an amazing economy, especially since the country has so many powerful multinational companies that helped them build the economy they have today. As a result, the country needs many different types of professionals, from analytical to creative. Given the high demand for skilled people, there are many jobs in Canada that must be filled, which makes getting a Canadian visa all the more worth it. 

Whether you are interested in a high-life career in one of the country’s most vibrant cities or want to live a more relaxed life in one of its inland territories, there is something for everyone, and a high-quality life to suit all of you and your family’s needs.

If you’ve ever thought that Canada was behind when it comes to getting the opportunity to work for a renowned company, think again. On the following list, you’ll find that many famous companies don’t only have offices in Canada, but are purely Canadian too!

Visit the Canadian government website for more information on the process of how to find a job in Canada today.


10 Famous Companies that are Canadian:


10. MAC Cosmetics

Founded in 1984, MAC Cosmetics is known as one of the most popular makeup brands in the world and has maintained its powerful presence in the market ever since. While you know MAC Cosmetics as the makeup store that you can find in nearly any shopping mall, what many people don’t know is that it is not an American company. The sophisticated makeup brand was started in Toronto, which is something that Canada can be proud of given its ongoing success globally. If you are interested in positions, such as artist in retail, buying, and more, there are many available all across Canada.

9. Herschel Supply Company

Founded in Vancouver in 2009, this incredibly successful backpack manufacturing company has products in stores worldwide. The brand involves the process of creating a product that spans across different style spaces. From celebrities, everyday workers, and travellers, their quality and aesthetic is appreciated by millions. The company has 44 retail stores worldwide and dozens of positions available in Canada ranging from photo editors to sales associates.

The opportunity to work in a young and dynamic environment needs to be seized today, Herschel Supply is known for its youthful culture and inspiring work environment.

8. Naked and Famous

Creating denim products that are popular around the globe, Naked and Famous specializes in what they term Japanese selvedge denim. Their hook is creating something different from what everyone knows to be the traditional pair of denim jeans, along with many other basic clothing pieces. There are hundreds of opportunities in high-end retail jobs across the country at designer brands like Puma, Brooks Brothers, Tommy Hilfiger, and many more.

7. IMAX

You may not know this depending on where in the world you live and how recently IMAX appeared in your country, but this popular entertainment brand has been around since the late sixties. Known for taking already big-screen motion pictures and putting them onto even bigger screens for an unforgettable cinematic experience, the IMAX brand will be a staple for years to come. The company, based in Mississauga, also manufactures cameras specifically for these large-format films to be shot with. From cashier to mechanical engineer, IMAX has positions available across the spectrum.


6. Thomson Reuters 

As one of the world’s leading media and journalism companies that was founded in 1954, this Toronto-based company has positioned itself as a global leader in the information, news, and professional tools industry with arms reaching into over 100 countries. Some positions at the multinational corporation include software engineers, editors, lawyers, and correspondents.

5. Fairmont Hotels & Resorts

With headquarters in Toronto and 75 properties scattered around the world, as one can imagine, a large luxury hotel chain as big as this one has a lot of administration, marketing, and hands-on positions that need to be filled. With so much work available, this is one of the most popular Canadian companies in the tourism industry to work for. Fairmont Hotels & Resorts is one of the most iconic hotels in the world and is specifically known for its famous The Plaza Hotel in New York and the Fairmont Monte Carlo and Monaco.

4. Four Seasons Hotels & Resorts

Popularly known as an elite hotel and founded in 1961, this is the behemoth of Canada’s hospitality industry. With 117 properties worldwide, there are many vacancies available in this major hotel chain that are waiting to be filled. From servers, housekeepers, concierges, barmen, admin, and management positions available.

3. Hootsuite

Anyone and everyone working in the marketing industry or managing social media know about Hootsuite. It’s a platform that helps you manage your social media posts, among many other elements of social media platforms, such as Facebook, Instagram, Twitter, LinkedIn, and Pinterest.

Headquartered in Vancouver, landing a position at this company would be the opportunity of a lifetime. Aside from working in a dynamic work environment, you would be living in one of the most exciting cities in the world, with an unrivaled countryside at your doorstep to be explored. People with IT skills and qualifications are highly in demand in Vancouver.


2. Barrick Gold Corporation

This Canadian company is the second-largest mining corporation in the world. Headquartered in Toronto, there are many jobs available at Barrick Gold Corporation, which ranges from unskilled mining labourers to skilled positions such as Engineers and Surveyors. There are a lot of in demand artisans finding very well paid work at Barrick’s sites around Canada, with positions available such as welders, millwrights, electricians and much more.

1. McCain

It is highly likely that no matter where you are in the world, you have enjoyed one of McCain’s delicious potato chips. Founded in New Brunswick in 1957, McCain has become a household name worldwide. As the world’s largest manufacturer of frozen potato and vegetable products, the company has over 19,000 staff members. With McCain being such a big company, there are always vacancies available. You could find work at McCain as a long-haul truck driver, dock worker, general laborer, customer care, and many many other opportunities

.

Moving to a new country should be an exciting and epic experience. Securing a job before arriving in Canada will allow you to go in with confidence to make this new country your new home.

How to Find a Job in Canada


Finding a job in Canada is easier than you think because there are so many big and incredibly popular options to choose from. Since Canada is continuing to expand its prospects, it’s branching out everything from retail stores, shopping centers, entertainment facilities, finance, design, marketing, and business companies. You name it, Canada’s got it, and most probably an employee spot for you too.

If you haven’t applied for a job or made plans to move to Canada yet, you can get in touch with a Canadian Regulated Immigration Consultant (RCICs), who can help you get a Canadian work visa to make a successful move.
