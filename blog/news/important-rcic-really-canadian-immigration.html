How Important Are Rcic Really For Your Canadian Immigration?

by Nicky VZL. Updated: March 15th, 2018

Would you feel safe flying with a pilot that is unlicensed? Probably not. The same thing applies for your immigration process. You want to feel safe and secure knowing your Visa application is in the right hands.

That is where Regulated Canadian Immigration Consultants (RCICs) come into the picture.

Who are RCICs?

RCICs are professional strategists that are qualified in Canadian Immigration Law. They are also in good standing with Immigration Refugees Citizenship Canada (IRCC). RCICs are authorized to give you up to date Canadian immigration advice and help you find the best route to take in order to achieve Canadian residency.

They are the Canadian immigration professionals who have studied and mastered the immigration process. Their mission? Giving you personalized assistance by mapping out the best immigration option for you.

WANT TO MAKE YOUR CANADIAN IMMIGRATION GO FASTER? HIRE AN RCIC

Scared of submitting the incorrect documents? Perhaps giving vague or irrelevant answers in your application form? Applying for the wrong visa?

MAXIMIZE YOUR CANADIAN IMMIGRATION CHANCES

Ensure that your Canadian immigration application is fully optimized for success by using a Regulated Canadian Immigration Consultant (RCIC) to handle your application form on your behalf.

An RCIC will give you step by step guidance during all of the immigration stages so that you are never left in the dark. Immigrating to Canada requires piles of paperwork that you must complete correctly, and with over sixty visa options to choose from, it can be very confusing. So, relying on an expert in the immigration field is a great choice, to save you time and money on failed applications.

Personalised Visa Roadmap

RCICs provide you with a beneficial strategy for your visa options. Thanks to a Personalized Visa Roadmap, an RCIC can assist you in making an educated and favourable choice for your future for what visa best suits you.

Deadlines

Submission of immigration documents are time sensitive. An RCIC will submit correctly compiled documents on your behalf, to ensure you meet immigration deadlines.

Canadianvisa.org has three expert RCICs to help you reach the Canadian dream. Apply today, talk to your RCIC soon!
