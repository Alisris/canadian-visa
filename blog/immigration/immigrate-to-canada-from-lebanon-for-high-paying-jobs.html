Immigrate to Canada from Lebanon for High Paying Jobs

by Robynn. Updated: January 14th, 2020

Canada is known to have an exceptionally high quality of life as well as being one of the most politically stable and secure countries in the world. It also has the 11th highest-ranking economy in the world and was voted as one of the top 10 safest countries to live in globally. Canada’s healthcare system is not only world-renowned for its high standards and accessibility but is also free to all of its citizens and permanent residents.

Along with free healthcare, when you immigrate to Canada from Lebanon, your children will also have access to free public education up until grade 12, making it the perfect place to raise a family. Thousands of skilled and semi-skilled workers receive invitations to immigrate to Canada every month, and the amount of projected newcomers to receive Invitations to Apply (ITAs) has increased from 340,000 in 2019 to 350,000 in 2020, which means more opportunities to get permanent residence in Canada.

Canada is one of the most dynamic, diverse and multicultural communities in the world. 7.7% of Canada's population is Muslim, and with a prominent and supportive Islamic ex-pat community helping you adapt to Canadian life with ease, it is no surprise that so many Lebanese are considering Canada as their destination of choice to start a new life abroad.

Canada is set to invite 1 million newcomers by 2021, and you could be one of them when you apply through one of over 70 immigration and visa options. The government has addressed the current gap in the labour market by creating programs such as the PNP (Provincial Nominee Program) and the Express Entry system to not only speed up the visa application process but to make it easier for you to immigrate to Canada.

Canada also has plenty of jobs available to you and your spouse, with an average earning potential of $55,800 per year. Among the top occupations in demand include physicians, registered nurses, engineers, IT workers and management positions, as well as in the education, manufacturing and tourism and hospitality sectors, which urgently need to be filled.

And one of the added benefits of becoming a permanent resident is that after just 3 years of living and working in Canada, you and your loved ones could be Canadian citizens.

Interested in speaking to one of our government trusted and accredited RCICs (Regulated Canadian Immigration Consultants) about your options to immigrate to Canada? Simply click the link below or keep reading to find out how you could take the next steps towards making a new life in Canada.

Immigrate To Canada From Lebanon: Top 3 Ways to Change Your Life


1. Save on Healthcare and Invest in Your Children’s Future.

As mentioned before, Canada has amazing benefits, such as its world-renowned public healthcare system, which comes at no cost to Canadian citizens and permanent residents as well as it’s phenomenal education system. These institutions feature among some of the top educational institutions in the world, which means that your children will be able to get a first-class secondary education free of charge!

By saving on years of secondary schooling and basic healthcare, you will be able to invest in your child’s future and save up for university tuition, allowing them to attend one of the world’s most highly regarded education institutions.


2. Study in Canada and Add Valuable Canadian Work Experience to Your Professional Resume.

Did you know that when you graduate from a DLI (Designated Learning Institution) in Canada, you may be eligible to apply to extend your stay and work in Canada through the PGWPP (Post Graduate Work Permit Program)? This allows you to gain valuable Canadian work experience in occupations with a NOC (National Occupation Classification) skill 0 or level A or B, which increases your chances of getting permanent residence in Canada through the CEC (Canadian Experience Class). After a mere 1 year of work experience, you may be eligible to apply for the CEC through the Express Entry system and could receive permanent residence in as little as 4 months!


3. Is Your Occupation In Demand? Choose the Right Province & Secure Permanent Residence in Canada.

Canada currently has thousands of jobs available for both semi and highly skilled workers, however, most individuals often make the mistake of assuming that it is easy to find employment in one of Canada’s popular provinces. The truth is that these areas receive an influx of residency and job applications, which means that your chances of being successful become highly competitive as you’ll be ranked against more skilled workers than those applying to relocate to smaller communities in Canada.

As a highly skilled person, you may have a great chance of receiving an ITA through the Express Entry system but this does not guarantee that you will find employment once you immigrate to Canada.

One of the best ways to immigrate to Canada is to apply for a visa through a Provincial Nominee Program that has an Express Entry category. It is important to ensure that your occupation is in demand in your selected Canadian territory or province. This will increase your chances of receiving a PN (Provincial Nomination) which is worth 600 extra CRS (Comprehensive Ranking System) points. Your maximum CRS score is 1200 which means that by having a PN you will already have half of the possible points required to receive an ITA.

This also optimizes your chance of finding a job offer, which is necessary to qualify to apply through the PNP. The more jobs available, the higher the chances that you will find a job and the higher your chances of getting permanent residence in Canada.

Another benefit to applying through a PNP with an Express Entry category is that having an Express Entry profile, allows a province/territory to select your profile from the Express Entry draw pools, which takes place every 2 weeks. This means that your chances to immigrate to Canada are greatly increased simply by having an Express Entry profile.
