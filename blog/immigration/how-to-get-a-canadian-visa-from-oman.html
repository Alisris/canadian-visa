How to Get a Canadian Visa from Oman

by Theresa. Updated: June 15th, 2021

If you are interested in living in a country that offers you the perfect setting to raise a family, or perhaps even to take your career to the next level, then Canada might be the place for you.

Offering many benefits, you’ll be happy to find that immigrating to Canada from Oman is easier than you may have initially imagined. As a socially and culturally diverse country, Canada, otherwise known as America’s Hat, offers diversity and is willing to extend opportunities to people from all over the world. 

While the process of getting a Canadian visa from Oman may seem lengthy and does take some time, there are many programs, such as those under the Express Entry system, introduced by the Immigration, Refugees, Citizenship Canada (IRCC), that break down and simplify the process of immigration. With this system as only one good example of getting to where you want to go, there are more ways than one to make a successful move. Besides, if you manage to get a successful career going, settle your family in a secure and relaxed environment, and are finally able to follow your dreams and meet the prospects you have initiated in your mind, then, why not?

The Best Canadian Immigration Routes for Omanis

In Canada, there is not one, not two, but over a hundred immigration programs to choose from, which means that there is very likely one that is perfect for you too. With some of these programs, like the Express Entry system, it’s also possible to immigrate to Canada in as little as six to eight months, making it the ideal option for anyone that is looking for a change.

How Express Entry Works

As a system that is the main driver of Canada’s economic growth, the Express Entry system is necessary for you to apply for immigration through some of Canada’s top immigration programs, including:

  • Federal Skilled Worker Program (FSWP)
  • Federal Skilled Trades Program (FSTP)
  • Canadian Experience Class

With an expected immigration target through the Express Entry system of just over 320,000 for the next three years, if you apply for one of these programs and meet the requirements, you are almost guaranteed to gain permanent residency in Canada. With this, you are also likely to get a job fast, due to the country’s need for skilled professionals in various fields.

By meeting all the necessary requirements, you build your Comprehensive Ranking System (CRS) score, which is made up by the following factors:

  • Human Capital Factors
  • Skill transferability
  • Spouse/ common-law partner
  • Additional factors

These factors take the following into account:

  • Age
  • Education qualifications
  • Work experience
  • Language skills
  • Adaptability
  • Job offers
  • Spouse or common-law partner


    • The first step for a successful application is to complete an online evaluation, followed by completing a language test in English, French, or both languages. If you would like to gain extra points, it is in your best interest to complete and pass both language tests, yet one will be efficient too if you meet the other requirements. You must also provide proof of a tertiary qualification(s), and work experience, which can be done by providing a letter from each of your previous employers, you must also provide information about the person you are married to or your common-law partner.

    Once you have completed these requirements, you will undergo a biometrics screening and have to find out which job National Occupation Classification (NOC) you belong to. Next, you must create your Express Entry profile. If you are interested in finding a job in Canada, you can also visit the Canadian Job Bank.

    The CRS score consists of 1,200 points, of which 600 are core points and 600 are additional points. Ideally, you need to score as high as possible to compete in the Express Entry Draw among a pool of applicants, which takes place every two weeks. Should you be selected, you will receive an Invitation to Apply (ITA), which means that you have been approved to accept an offer of permanent residency that you need to accept within six months to get a visa to move to Canada.

    Why Immigrate to Canada?


    There are more than a handful of reasons why people simply love to immigrate to live in Canada. Ask anyone, whether it is a native or foreigner that has lived in Canada for a while, no matter where you are from, Canada is just one of those countries that are bound to grow on you. Is it the snow or the local animals that can’t seem to be found anywhere else in the world? Or, is there a bigger reason why it has been rated the number one country with the best quality of life?

    Well, when it comes to living, whether you measure living a good life with variables such as making good money, having peace around you, a sound political system, or simply the fact that the country has over 250 ethnic cultures living in the country, take your pick, Canada offers it all.

    Top reasons to move to Canada:

    1. Beautiful cities

      Canada has one of the most beautiful cities in the world, which is Toronto, named the fourth most beautiful city in the entire world. With its high quality of life rating, many factors are taken into account, including safety, technology, health, education, transport, ease of doing business, and living your most free life. Cities such as Montreal, Quebec, Calgary, Vancouver, and Ontario are all ranked extremely high for meeting these factors.

    2. Incredible people

      One of the biggest reasons why Canada is considered a terrific place to raise a family or find a job that you are happy in, is because people in Canada are very friendly and warm individuals. That means that not only will your children and entire family find it easy to adapt to Canada, but they will enjoy it. In the work environment, you will also find that the people around you are supportive and want you to win. Since Canada is also known for having some of the most friendly people in the world, it’s no doubt that making friends and building a new life can be a walk in the park.

    3. Freedom and equality beyond compare

      In the world, things are a little shaky at the moment. Whether it’s the Covid-19 pandemic that makes us feel like we can’t move forward or grow in life or the injustices taking place globally, perhaps one of the biggest reasons why Canada is the ideal place to be, is because it’s so free. People are extremely accepting of each other and appreciate foreigners for moving to the country to strengthen the country. Every person, no matter what color, race, or religion, is also accepted as a person in Canada because everybody is valued as humans first and foremost, equally.

    4. Entertainment is alive and well

      When it comes to enjoying life, apart from the stress-free feeling that Canada gives you, there are countless wide-open spaces that you can visit in the country. In this regard, Canada has a little of everything, including oceans, countryside, mountains, forests, and, of course, some of the best cities for entertainment globally. From cultural diversity in food, music, and fun activities in every province, you will never experience a dull moment.

      5. Are You Ready to Move to Canada?


      While immigrating to a different country can seem difficult, you can do so easily by following the necessary steps and taking action today to create your ideal future tomorrow.

      If you are ready to move to Canada, you can learn more about how to immigrate to Canada from Oman by consulting a Regulated Canadian Immigration Consultant (RCIC).

      If Canada has ever seemed out of reach to you before, you can rest assured that your opportunity to immigrate and work in Canada can happen for you in as little as six months!
