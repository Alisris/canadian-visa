Why Immigrate to Canada

Employment opportunities

Through immigration streams such as the Provincial Nominee Program and the Express Entry Program, Canada is opening up hundreds of thousands of job opportunities to foreigners.   • Haulage drivers
  • Lab Technicians
  • Electricians

  • Mining and Metallurgical Engineers

Permanent Residency

There are a number of routes to becoming a permanent resident in Canada. For those with university qualifications and experience in in-demand occupations, there is the Express Entry program. If you are a semi-skilled worker there are many other pathways to PR status in Canada such as the Atlantic Immigration Pilot and the Agri-Food Immigration Pilot. There are strict requirements that must be met for all of these programs, and if you do you have a chance of becoming a citizen in a country with one of the best social service systems on earth. Keep in mind that public services in Canada includes free healthcare and schooling for your children, and for a glimpse at how well Canada treats its residents and citizens, have a look at there Covid-19 response.

You Donâ€™t Have to Leave Your Family Behind

Canada is serious about keeping families together. If you meet the minimum settlement fund requirements, you can bring your family with you.

Immigrants often worry about how they will be able to adjust, whether they will be accepted into communities and if they may face prejudices. Canada however is a multicultural hub, welcoming thousands of new immigrants every month, it really is one of the easiest societies to integrate into because as it stands, almost 25% of the population are immigrants. On top of that, a recent survey showed over 90% of Canadians would welcome someone of a different culture into their community. For a little more depth into what life in Canada is like, click here.

How Do I Get To Canada?

There are a number of various immigration pathways and streams that will help you on your pathway to becoming a permanent resident and even a Canadian citizen one day.
